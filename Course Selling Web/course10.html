What You'll Learn

Learn advanced tools and frameworks for developing competitive business strategies. Through case studies, discussions, and group exercise, you'll deepen your understanding of market and cost dynamics, varied business models, and how to maximize your strategic advantage to disrupt the competition.


Neither your competitors, nor your colleagues, can make important business decisions with 100% certainty. What successful leaders need is a strong understanding of how to develop a strong, competitive business strategy and strategic analysis skills to make confident decisions. This advanced business strategy program is designed to equip you with these skills so you can navigate critical strategic decisions during a pivotal point in your career.


The advanced business strategy program is ideal for a wide range of individuals, including senior leaders, leaders transitioning into new roles, and strategy and finance professionals.
